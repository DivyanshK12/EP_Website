About Us

Welcome to the Department of Physics of IIT Hyderabad! Our Department has 23 full time faculty members leading research groups which are making groundbreaking contributions (hyperlinked to awards, achievements of faculty and research scholars) in diverse areas. Our department hosts world class research programs in fields of Astrophysics and Cosmology, Computational Condensed Matter Physics, Experimental Condensed Matter Physics, High Energy Physics and Optics, Laser and Spectroscopy.

Our Department is strongly driven by our mission

  • to figure out how things work and how the universe is put together at all scales
  • to solve technological problems & invent new technologies which benefit the society at large
  • to deliver a world-class education based on rigorous technical training to our students and scholars

Our Department is equipped with state of the art laboratories, a 14-inch telescope and organizes highly topical workshops and conferences, and boasts of several international and multidisciplinary collaborations (hyperlinks). We also offer several degree programs for aspiring physicists and engineers

  • Bachelors (BTech) in Engineering Physics
  • Masters (MSc) in Physics
  • Doctorate (PhD) in Physics

If you are an undergraduate student in science, technology, or engineering, aspiring to be an expert in solving real world problems which affect the local community, the nation and global community, consider joining us
